About this game

"Snake" is a classic arcade game where players control a snake, aiming to collect food (usually represented by dots or fruits) to increase their length. The challenge lies in avoiding collisions with the snake's own tail. As the snake grows, the game becomes more challenging, adding to the excitement. The objective is to score as many points as possible by surviving for as long as possible.
