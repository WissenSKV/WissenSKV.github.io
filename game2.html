About this game

"Memory Game" is a classic game where the objective is to uncover pairs of matching cards displayed on a game board. Players flip over two cards at a time, and if they match, the cards remain face-up. If they don't match, the cards are flipped back, and the player must remember their positions for future attempts. The game typically includes various difficulty levels with an increasing number of cards, making the task more challenging. The goal is to uncover all pairs with the fewest moves possible. "Memory Game" enhances memory, attention, and concentration, providing an engaging experience that exercises cognitive skills.
